FAQ

Here are the
essentials.

What makes your barrier technology different? +

Unlike traditional wax or plastic coatings, our proprietary polymer matrix is 100% biodegradable and actively reacts to root exudates, meaning it only breaks down when the plant specifically signals for nutrients.

How does it impact soil health? +

It improves it. Because the casing breaks down into benign, organic elements, it acts as a very mild carbon source for soil microbes rather than leaving behind harmful microplastics.

Can this scale to commercial farming? +

Yes. Our North American manufacturing plant is already producing at a scale capable of supplying thousands of acres, seamlessly integrating with standard industrial spreading equipment.

What are the storage requirements? +

Store in a cool, dry place. The barrier technology is highly stable in low-moisture environments and only activates upon sustained contact with active soil biology.
